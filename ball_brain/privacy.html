Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is designed to inform users of the Ball Brain mobile application ("App" or "Service") about our practices concerning the collection, utilization, storage, and disclosure of Personal Information when you access or use our Service. By downloading, installing, or using Ball Brain, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. The terms defined herein shall have the same interpretation as in our Terms of Service for Ball Brain, unless otherwise specified.

1. Information We Collect

We only gather information that is essential for enhancing your experience with Ball Brain, ensuring the App’s functionality, and delivering relevant services. The types of information we may collect include:

         Account-related details: When you log in to the App for the first time using your Google account, we will receive your email address, full name, and profile picture associated with that account. This information is used solely to create and manage your unique user account within the App.

         Advertising Identifier (IDFA/GAID): We collect your device’s Advertising Identifier (such as IDFA for iOS devices or GAID for Android devices). This identifier helps us collaborate with third-party ad providers to deliver targeted, non-intrusive ads within the App and to detect and prevent fraudulent activities that could harm the App’s ecosystem.

         IP Address: Your device’s Internet Protocol (IP) address is collected to identify your general geographic location (e.g., country or region) and to ensure the security and stability of the App’s servers during your usage.

         Device specifications: We gather basic information about the device you use to access Ball Sort Flow, including the device model, operating system version, screen resolution and display settings, system language, time zone, and the timestamp of your App usage. This data helps us optimize the App’s performance for different devices.

         Log Data: In the event of an App crash, error, or unexpected behavior, we automatically collect Log Data. This data includes details such as the actions you were performing in the App before the issue occurred, the App’s configuration at that time, and error codes. Log Data is critical for diagnosing problems, fixing bugs, and continuously improving the App’s reliability.

         Installed Apps (with user consent): The App integrates the Unity Ads SDK to display video ads. At the App’s launch, the Unity Ads SDK may request permission to access information about the other apps installed on your device. You have the right to deny this request; if you do, it will not affect your ability to use the core features of Ball Brain. Importantly, Ball Brain’s developer does not have access to this installed apps information—only the Unity Ads SDK may process it, in accordance with Unity’s own privacy policies.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively to support and enhance your experience with Ball Brain, and we never use it for purposes unrelated to the App’s functionality. Our key uses of your information include:

         Delivering and maintaining the Service: Your account details (e.g., email) are used to verify your identity, track your progress in the App (such as level completion and game preferences), and manage any in-app rewards or achievements you earn. When processing rewards (if applicable), we may use your linked payment account information (provided by you) to ensure you receive the rewards you are entitled to. Your Advertising Identifier is used indirectly (as required by third-party ad providers) to display ads and relevant in-app tasks.

         Communicating with you: We may use your email address to contact you in specific situations, such as when you submit a request to delete your data (to confirm the request’s status) or when there are significant updates to this Privacy Policy or the App’s Terms of Service (to ensure you are informed of changes that may affect your usage).

         Providing customer support: If you reach out to us with questions, concerns, or reports of technical issues (e.g., bugs or glitches), we will use your email address to respond to your inquiries and investigate the issues you’ve raised. This helps us resolve problems efficiently and improve your overall experience.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We prioritize the security and confidentiality of your information and only disclose it in limited, legally permissible circumstances:

         Third-party ad providers: Your Advertising Identifier (IDFA/GAID) may be shared with trusted third-party ad providers (e.g., Unity Ads). This disclosure is necessary for these providers to deliver targeted ads within the App and to ensure that ads are displayed correctly. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and may not use it for any purpose other than providing ad-related services for Ball Brain.

         Business transactions: In the event of a corporate transaction such as a merger, acquisition, sale of assets, or bankruptcy, your information may be disclosed to the involved third parties as part of the due diligence or evaluation process. However, we will ensure that the receiving party agrees to uphold the same level of privacy protection as outlined in this Policy.

         Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a valid court order, subpoena, or government request. We will only disclose the minimum amount of information necessary to comply with such legal obligations.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable and industry-standard measures to safeguard your Personal Information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure:

         Encryption: All information transmitted between your device and our servers (e.g., account details, Log Data) is encrypted using Transport Layer Security (TLS) protocol. Additionally, any information stored on our servers (at rest) is encrypted to prevent unauthorized access.

         Access controls: We implement strict technical and administrative controls to limit access to your nonpublic Personal Information. Only authorized employees, contractors, or service providers (who have signed confidentiality agreements) may access this information, and only for purposes related to operating the App.

         Security updates: We regularly update our App and server systems to address potential security vulnerabilities and protect against emerging threats. However, please note that no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we strive to minimize risks to your information.

5. Data Retention

We retain your Personal Information only for as long as it is necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law:

         General retention: Most of your information (e.g., device specifications, IP address) is retained until it is no longer needed to improve the App’s performance, resolve issues, or comply with legal obligations.

         Data deletion requests: If you submit a request to delete your account and related data, we will promptly delete your personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, and profile picture) once your request is approved. However, in limited cases (e.g., to prevent duplicate new-user rewards, as outlined in our Terms of Service), we may retain certain non-identifiable information (such as payment account identifiers) until we confirm that it is no longer needed to enforce our Terms of Service.

         Legal retention: We may retain your information for a longer period if required by law (e.g., to comply with tax or record-keeping regulations).

6. How to Request Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your Ball Brain account and all associated data, you may do so through one of the following methods:

  1. In-App request: Navigate to the "Feedback" or "Account Settings" page within the App, select the "Request Data Deletion" option, and follow the on-screen instructions to submit your request.
  2. Email request: Send an email to our support team at hanpeicui@gmail.com with the subject line "Ball Brain Data Deletion Request." Please include your registered email address (linked to your Google account) in the email to help us identify and process your request efficiently. We will confirm receipt of your request within 3 business days and complete the deletion process within 14 business days, unless additional time is required for legal or technical reasons.

7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that act as anonymous unique identifiers. Ball Brain does not use cookies directly. However, the App may integrate third-party code, libraries, or SDKs (e.g., ad providers, analytics tools) that use cookies or similar technologies (such as device fingerprints) to collect information about your App usage. These tools help third parties improve their services (e.g., optimizing ad delivery) and analyze how users interact with the App.

         Your choices: You can manage or disable cookies through your device’s settings. For example, on most mobile devices, you can adjust privacy settings to block third-party cookies. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain third-party features (e.g., targeted ads) within the App, but it will not impact your ability to use the core game features of Ball Sort Flow.

         Contact list access: With your explicit permission, we may upload your device’s contact list to our servers for the sole purpose of fraud detection (e.g., preventing multiple accounts from being created using the same contact information) and contact synchronization (if you choose to use a feature that allows you to connect with friends via the App). We will not use your contact list for marketing purposes without additional consent.
